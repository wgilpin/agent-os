|----------|-------------|

| effector → StateStore | The effector is the sole caller of `apply_action`; this enforces the single-writer + ring-split shape at v0. |

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |

NOT full enforcement — the deterministic gate (DEC-deterministic-gate-is-the-firewall) is Phase 3; but the drop-and-log shape pre-stages it. Verified by ungranted-action-dropped test. |
